About YOSH UMPSA

Welcome to YOSH UMPSA, the most happening club on campus! We're a diverse community of students passionate about creating memorable experiences and fostering connections.

We've organized multiple events ranging from volunteering and outdoor adventures. Our mission is simple: to make university life more exciting, meaningful, and fun!

Whether you're looking to develop new skills, meet like-minded people or just have a good time. YOSH UMPSA has something for everyone. Join us and be part of something amazing!
